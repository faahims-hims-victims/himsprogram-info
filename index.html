✈️ Pilots Deserve a Fair Path Forward

We're a grassroots initiative advocating for all pilots impacted by FAA psychiatric monitoring—whether due to alcohol or drug use, SSRI or other prescription medications, mental health history, or even unfounded suspicion without medical diagnosis.

We believe in transparency, fairness, and support—not fear, coercion, or silence. New to HIMS? Start with our New Pilot Guide. Under pressure right now? See the Emergency Toolkit. Have questions? Check our FAQ.
